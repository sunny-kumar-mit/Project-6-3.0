Fresh Storage Guide

Ideal temperatures for fruits & vegetables

General Refrigerator Temperature

Main fridge compartment: 0°C to 4°C (32°F to 39°F)

Storage Tips

  • Use the crisper drawer for veggies and fruits — it keeps humidity higher.
  • Separate fruits and vegetables when possible because many fruits release ethylene gas that speeds up ripening (and spoiling) of vegetables.
  • Store leafy greens in plastic bags or containers to maintain humidity.
  • Keep ethylene-producing fruits (like apples) away from sensitive vegetables.
